APPLICATION DO’S & DON’TS FOR APPLICATION



The Do’s

1. Check that you are eligible to apply!

• Applicant should be a student of Botho University / University of Botswana / Botswana Accountancy College / Botswana University of Science and Technology.

• Have a business idea that is solving a certain problem.

• Have a business idea that is solving a certain problem.

• Only applicants who meet the criteria will have their application reviewed.

2. Be honest. If you are shortlisted to become a finalist, you will have to undergo rigorous due diligence by judges.

3. Demonstrate your answers. Where relevant provide evidence-based examples and details that will allow judges to better understand your points.

4. Answer all required questions. Required questions will be indicated with an asterix (*). Please note that incomplete applications will not be considered.

5. Please keep your answers short and clear. Inspire us with your business but do so within the word limits.

6. Submit your application on time. The most successful applicants submit ahead of the deadline and with enough time to have peers/mentors review their application. Quality matters and that takes time. We recommend you aim to complete your application at least a week before the deadline so you have time to make any edits required.

7. Get support, if you’re stuck. Visit your University contact person for real-time support, or TheNeoHub Facebook pages and email: build@theneohub.com

The Don’ts

Do not disclose commercial or security sensitive information.

Do not miss the deadline!! All applications must be submitted September 2022 23:59:59 GMT.

Do not overly elaborate/complicate your answers.

Do not apply as an individual, only teams are allowed into this challenge

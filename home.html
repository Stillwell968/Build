About us

BUILD is a competition management platform that gives businesses the opportunity to:

Create competitions online that are open to participants from multiple locations.

Engage, mentor and train participants..

Have access to a unique and customizable platform that they can tailor to their needs and wants.

Key features

BUILD platform allows you to:

Manage teams

Add teams manually or open an online registration page in minutes

Pick your format

Combine groups and brackets to create your idea competition setup

Create timelines

Plan competition schedule automatically using our scheduling tool and simply drag and drop to make changes

Automate Judging Processes

Process results effortlessly from any device and everyone is up to date
